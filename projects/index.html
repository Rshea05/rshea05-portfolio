AI Product Workflows

An AI-enabled product delivery framework that connects discovery, requirements, design, and engineering into a structured workflow using Claude, Notion, Figma, and Jira to accelerate enterprise product development.

Problem
Product teams were spending too much time rewriting requirements, documenting decisions, and translating work between product, design, and engineering. AI tools could generate content, but lacked the structure, governance, and review process needed for enterprise delivery.
Strategy
Designed a repeatable AI workflow centered on Claude for reasoning and content generation, Notion for structured knowledge management, and Figma for rapid concept exploration, with standardized prompts, reusable templates, and human review checkpoints.
Outcome
Reduced time spent creating product documentation and early design concepts while improving consistency across discovery and delivery, establishing AI as a trusted collaborator without sacrificing quality or oversight.

Carbon Capture Credit Service Blueprint

A multi-sprint service blueprint documenting a year-plus grower journey across dealers, Bayer teams, systems, seasonal delays, verification steps, and final compensation.

Problem

Wearable product concept / Youth athletic development

StrideNode

A wearable training system concept that helps youth soccer coaches measure acceleration, deceleration, balance, directional change, movement efficiency, and consistency over time.

Challenge
Youth athletes are often evaluated by visible outcomes like goals, size, and speed, while off-ball movement skills and long-term development patterns remain hard to measure consistently.
Concept
Player-worn sensors capture GNSS and motion data during repeatable sprint and agility drills, then send results to a local Raspberry Pi hub with a coach-facing dashboard.
Vision
Give coaches, players, and families a private, growth-centered view of movement development without turning children into public rankings.
