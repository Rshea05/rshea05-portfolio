How I Work

A practical product design process, built for cross-functional teams.

  1. 1

    Frame the problem

    Clarify user needs, business goals, constraints, and the decision points that matter.

  2. 2

    Structure the work

    Translate ambiguity into flows, requirements, reusable patterns, and shared artifacts.

  3. 3

    Align and iterate

    Partner with product and engineering to refine decisions and reduce delivery friction.

  4. 4

    Ship repeatable quality

    Build systems and documentation that make the next product decision easier than the last.
